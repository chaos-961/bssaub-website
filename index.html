Story · Mission · Vision

Our Journey

  1. Since 1965

    Our Story

    Founded in 1965 as the Commerce Students' Society, BSS began with a simple idea: what we learn in class becomes transformative when applied.

    Today it is a dynamic hub led by students across finance, marketing, consulting, entrepreneurship, and management. Through events, partnerships, and student initiatives, BSS keeps expanding its impact, true to one belief: students learn best when challenged, connected, and inspired.

  2. What drives us

    Our Mission

    BSS exists to empower business students to grow beyond the classroom, bridging theory and practice through professional exposure, leadership opportunities, and a community built on learning, collaboration, and career growth.

    Through events, workshops, competitions, and partnerships, we help students build skills for the real world, explore diverse business paths, and connect with industry professionals.

  3. Where we're headed

    Our Vision

    We envision a student body that is confident, capable, and ready for their careers.

    BSS strives to become the leading platform for business students, fostering initiative, professionalism, and impact, and preparing members to thrive in an evolving global business environment.
